Internship Program

// Summer 2019

//internship overview

ITITITinc invites applications for its inaugural Summer internship program rom qualified undergraduate students for its project ​cosa (c​ommunally organized social architecture). cosa combines aspects of IoT, blockchain and smart contracts,interaction and UXdesign, and draws on robust cultural theory. As a hardware/software ecosystem for micro-fundraising and micro-entrepreneurship, cosa looks towards a new generation of payment systems and labor/resource valuation.

The ITITIT internship provides opportunities for undergraduate students to participate in a start-up culture with a heavy critical theoretical focus. Students will work in small teams on projects timetabled for an entire quarter (Summer 2019). ITITITinc emphasizes a pedagogy of project-driven and enacted learning in combination with a culture of mentorship and apprenticing.

Intern teams will be supervised by the ITITIT team, many of whom hold or are pursuing PhDs and Masters in interactive systems, technology and society, arts and design, and computer science. In addition to this interaction, advanced interns will have the opportunity to meet with team members for more rigorous mentorship.

ITITITinc is seeking interns interested in learning or developing proficiency in the following areas:

  • 3D modeling and fabrication;
  • app development (SwiftOS, Java (Android));
  • physical digital systems
  • internet of things
  • blockchain
  • photography and graphic design
  • web development
